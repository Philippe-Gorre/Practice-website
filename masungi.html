Masungi Georeserve: A Rainforest Sanctuary

Adventure and Conservation Amidst 60-Million-Year-Old Limestone.

Masungi Georeserve is a world-class conservation project that offers an adventure unlike any other in Southeast Asia. Tucked away in the rainforests of Baras, it is famous for its rugged karst landscapes and the innovative "Discovery Trail."

Quick Info:
Location:

Kilometer 47, Marcos Highway, Baras, Rizal.

Best for: Adventure Seekers, Eco-Warriors, and Nature Photographers.
Vibe: Wild, High-Adrenaline, and Pristine.


The trail allows visitors to traverse the landscape using hanging bridges and giant rope hammocks, such as the Sapot (Spiderweb), which hangs directly over jagged rocks. The project’s mission is to protect local wildlife and restore the forest from illegal logging and quarrying. As you walk, you learn about the delicate balance of the ecosystem and the importance of preserving our natural heritage. The experience is physically challenging but deeply rewarding, offering unparalleled views of the Sierra Madre and the satisfaction of supporting a vital environmental cause.
