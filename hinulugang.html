Hinulugang Taktak

Hinulugang Taktak: A Legend Reborn

From a Classic Summer Getaway to a Modern Adventure Park.

Hinulugang Taktak is a name that resonates with Filipino history. Once the premier weekend destination of the 1950s, it has been revitalized into a National Historical Landmark that blends nostalgic beauty with modern adventure.

Quick Info:
Location:

Taktak Rd., Antipolo, Rizal.

Best for: Families, Day-Trippers, and Recreational Climbers.
Vibe: Nostalgic, Active, and Community-Focused.


The 70-foot waterfall remains the park's centerpiece, but today it is surrounded by new facilities like a climbing wall, a spider web bridge, and a public swimming pool. It serves as a vital green space in the heart of Antipolo, where visitors can enjoy picnics or engage in outdoor sports. The park is a testament to environmental restoration, showing how a historic natural site can be preserved and updated for a new generation. It’s a place to revisit the past while enjoying the active, healthy lifestyle of the present.
