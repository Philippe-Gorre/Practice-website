St. Jerome Parish Church

St. Jerome Parish: The Morong Masterpiece

Admire One of the Most Spectacular Neo-Baroque Towers in the Philippines.

The Antipolo Cathedral is the spiritual heart of the province, home to the centuries-old statue of the Our Lady of Peace and Good Voyage. It is an international shrine that has drawn millions of devotees for over 400 years.

Quick Info:
Location:

P. Oliveros St., Antipolo, Rizal.

Best for: Architecture Lovers, History Buffs, and Artists.
Vibe: Grand, Ornate, and Cultural.


The church is perched on a hill, its three-story belfry serving as a landmark for miles. The tower is a fusion of Spanish Baroque and Chinese influences, with detailed carvings that include dragon-like motifs. It is widely considered one of the most beautiful belfries in the country. Inside, the church maintains a sense of colonial grandeur. It has served as a center of town life for generations, surviving through the centuries as a monument to the artistic and spiritual heritage of the people of Morong.
