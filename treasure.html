Treasure Mountain

Treasure Mountain: The Island in the Sky

Experience the Magic of the Clouds Without the Strenuous Climb.

Treasure Mountain has redefined mountain tourism by making the majestic "sea of clouds" accessible to everyone. Located in the highlands of Tanay, this private peak offers a breathtaking escape for those who want the view without the hike.

Quick Info:
Location:

Sitio Maysawa, Brgy. Cuyambay, Tanay, Rizal.

Best for: Families, Casual Travelers, and Overnight Campers.
Vibe: Relaxing, Dreamy, and Instagrammable.


The site is a favorite for visitors who want to wake up to a blanket of white fog rolling over the hills. Because it is accessible by vehicle, it has become a top choice for family reunions and quick weekend getaways. Beyond the clouds, the site features "Spider's Web" rope courses and an obstacle track for those who want a bit of a thrill. As the fog clears, the landscape reveals a stunning panoramic view of the Sierra Madre. Whether you are staying in a tent or just visiting for the day, Treasure Mountain offers a peaceful atmosphere where you can breathe the cool mountain air and watch the world wake up from above.
