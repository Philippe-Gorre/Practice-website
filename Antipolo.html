Antipolo Cathedral: The Pilgrimage Capital

A Sanctuary of Faith, Tradition, and Safe Travels.

The Antipolo Cathedral is the spiritual heart of the province, home to the centuries-old statue of the Our Lady of Peace and Good Voyage. It is an international shrine that has drawn millions of devotees for over 400 years.

Quick Info:
Location:

P. Oliveros St., Antipolo, Rizal.

Best for: Pilgrims, History Lovers, and Foodies.
Vibe: Sacred, Bustling, and Culturally Rich.


The cathedral is a center of deep devotion, especially during the month of May when thousands participate in the "Alay Lakad" (walking pilgrimage). It is also a tradition for many Filipinos to bring their new cars here for a blessing before a long journey. The area around the church is just as famous, known for its bustling market where you can buy Antipolo’s signature suman, cashews, and alamang. A visit to the cathedral is not just a religious experience but a dive into the vibrant local culture and traditions of Rizal.
