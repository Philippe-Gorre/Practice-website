Daranak Falls: Rizal’s Natural Oasis

Refresh Your Soul in the Cool, Turquoise Basins of Tanay.

TDaranak Falls is perhaps the most iconic waterfall in the province, famous for its 14-meter drop and the vibrant turquoise color of its natural pool. It has been a beloved summer destination for decades, offering a refreshing escape from the tropical heat.

Quick Info:
Location:

Brgy. Tandang Kutyo, Tanay, Rizal.

Best for: Families, Swimmers, and Picnic Lovers.
Vibe: Refreshing, Iconic, and Lush.


Surrounded by forest and natural rock formations, the falls provide a picturesque setting that has been featured in countless local films. The water is cold and clear, flowing from the mountains of the Sierra Madre. The park is well-equipped with picnic huts and stone paths, making it a comfortable destination for groups. For those looking for a quieter experience, a short trail leads to the nearby Batlag Falls, which is often less crowded. Daranak remains a symbol of Rizal’s natural abundance, offering a timeless experience of swimming in the heart of the jungle.
