Mount Daraitan: The Jewel of the Sierra Madre

A Rugged Ascent to One of the Philippines' Most Rewarding Summits.

Mount Daraitan is the ultimate challenge for hikers seeking a raw, untamed mountain experience just hours away from the city. Known for its steep limestone trails and sweeping 360-degree views, it stands as a testament to the rugged beauty of the Rizal highlands.

Quick Info:
Location:

Brgy. Daraitan, Tanay, Rizal.

Best for: Experienced Hikers, Fitness Buffs, and Summit Seekers.
Vibe: Challenging, Majestic, and Adventurous.


The climb is a test of endurance, featuring sharp rocks and dense forest cover that keep even seasoned trekkers on their toes. However, once you reach the summit, the struggle is instantly forgotten. The "Sea of Clouds" often blankets the valley in the early morning, revealing the winding Agos River and the blue ridges of the Sierra Madre as the sun rises. The mountain's unique limestone formations provide a dramatic backdrop for photos, while the descent leads directly to the cooling waters of the Tinipak River. It is a destination that balances a grueling physical feat with the serene tranquility of the clouds.
