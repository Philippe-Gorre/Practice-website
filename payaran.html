Payaran Falls

Payaran Falls: The Seven-Layered Secret

A Hidden Mountain Trek to Nature’s Infinity Pools.

Payaran Falls is a hidden gem tucked deep within the mountains of Rodriguez. It features a system of seven cascading waterfalls, each offering a unique natural pool for those willing to trek through the river and forest.

Quick Info:
Location:

Brgy. San Jose, Rodriguez (Montalban), Rizal.

Best for: Off-the-Beaten-Path Explorers and River Trekkers.
Vibe: Wild, Hidden, and Refreshing.


This is a destination for the true adventurer. Reaching the upper tiers requires crossing the river multiple times and climbing over rocky sections. Each level reveals a new waterfall, with the higher tiers acting like nature’s own infinity pools overlooking the forest. Because it is less developed than other falls in the province, Payaran offers a sense of solitude and raw beauty. It’s a place where you can hear nothing but the sound of rushing water and the rustle of leaves, providing a perfect escape for those looking to disconnect.
